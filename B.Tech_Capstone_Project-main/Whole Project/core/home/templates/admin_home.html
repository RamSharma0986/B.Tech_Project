What is Legal Dump?

A dump image refers to a visual representation of a waste disposal site, typically showing discarded materials piled up or scattered in an unorganized manner. It may depict various types of waste, including household garbage, industrial byproducts, or construction debris.


What is Illegal Dump?

A legal dump image represents a waste disposal site where materials are discarded in compliance with environmental regulations and local laws. These sites may include designated landfills, recycling centers, or other authorized facilities where waste is managed and processed responsibly to minimize environmental impact.
